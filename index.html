Sermon Keeper

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Sermon Keeper ("the App") is a personal productivity app for managing and organizing sermons. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Sermon Keeper stores all your data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. This includes:

  • Sermon titles, content, and notes
  • Folders and tags
  • Delivery dates and locations
  • App settings and preferences

2. Internet Access

The App may access the internet only for the following optional features:

  • E. G. White Writings — fetches content from the Ellen G. White Estate public API (egwwritings.org). No personal data is sent.
  • Bible verses — may fetch from a public Bible API. No personal data is sent.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties. The App does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

4. Permissions

  • Microphone — used only for the optional voice dictation feature, when you explicitly activate it. Audio is processed on-device.
  • Internet — used only for the optional EGW Writings and Bible verse features described above.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own protection (lock screen, encryption). We recommend keeping your device secured.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
